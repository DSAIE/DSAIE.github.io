About

DSAIE aims to showcase the transformative potential of Machine Intelligence and Data Science (MINDS) across diverse domains. Renowned speakers from academia, industry, and enterprise will illuminate how MI & DS theories & technologies are addressing real-world challenges and shaping the future:

Academia

Research in MINDS serves as the backbone of technological progress, driving advancements that redefine knowledge, industries and improve lives. Discussions in this area aim to explore breakthroughs in foundational theories, technologies, methods and experiments.

Industry

The transformative power of MINDS lies in their ability to revolutionize real-world applications, turning complex problems into actionable insights. This focus area emphasizes the practical integration of AI in domains such as e-commerce, healthcare, finance, agriculture, sustainability, etc.

Enterprise

Enterprise and Start-up represent the agile and creative frontier of MINDS, often spearheading bold ideas that reshape industries. This focus area celebrates entrepreneurial journeys, highlighting the risks, rewards, and resilience required to thrive in a competitive ecosystem.
